From patients, survivors, and caregivers just like you

During Treatment

Why am I so tired and exhausted during chemotherapy?

This is completely normal and one of the most common concerns we hear. Chemotherapy affects rapidly dividing cells throughout your body, and your immune system is working overtime to heal and protect you.

The fatigue you're experiencing is your body's way of telling you it needs rest to repair. Many patients describe it as unlike any tiredness they've felt before.

Our Therapeutic Energy Healing and Energy Aromatherapy are specifically designed to help restore your energy levels during treatment.

What can I do about nausea during chemo?

You're not alone in this struggle. Nausea is one of the most challenging side effects, affecting your ability to eat, maintain strength, and continue treatment.

Our AYUSH-certified aromatherapy blends include specific formulations that many patients find helpful for managing nausea alongside their prescribed anti-nausea medications.

Learn About Aromatherapy
I can't sleep during treatment. Is this normal?

Yes, sleep disturbances during cancer treatment are extremely common. Anxiety, medication side effects, and physical discomfort all contribute to disrupted sleep patterns.

Our Energy Psychotherapy addresses the emotional factors affecting your sleep, while our calming aromatherapy blends can help create a more restful environment.

Learn About Psychotherapy
How do I stay positive when treatment feels endless?

This feeling is valid and shared by many patients. Treatment can feel like a marathon with no clear finish line.

Our Community Support Group connects you with others who understand exactly what you're going through. Sometimes, just knowing you're not alone makes all the difference.

I'm scared I won't complete my treatment because of side effects.

This fear is more common than you might think. 30-50% of cancer patients experience treatment interruptions due to side effects.

This is exactly why we exist. Our comprehensive approach is designed to help you maintain your strength and continue your treatment as prescribed. We've helped patients with severe side effects complete their full treatment protocols.

After Treatment

I finished chemo but still feel exhausted. When will I feel normal?

Post-treatment fatigue is real and can last weeks to months. Your body has been through an enormous battle and needs time to recover.

"Normal" may look different now, but you will regain your strength. Our Energy Psychotherapy and Community Support programs are specifically designed for survivors rebuilding their lives.

Book Recovery Consultation
Why do I feel anxious now that treatment is over?

This is surprisingly common. During treatment, you had regular monitoring and a clear plan. Now, many survivors feel vulnerable without that structure.

Our Energy Psychotherapy helps you process these feelings and build confidence in your new normal.

How do I rebuild my strength after chemotherapy?

Recovery is a gradual process that involves your physical, emotional, and spiritual wellbeing. There's no rush.

Our Restorative Healing Retreat offers a 3-day immersion experience specifically designed for post-treatment recovery, helping you reconnect with yourself and rediscover your purpose.
